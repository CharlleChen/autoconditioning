Your air-conditioner is monitored and controlled automatically

Check My AC
AI-featured

Empowered by trained machine learning algorithms that detect human face and body, Autocolding provides accurate feedback on the number of people in the room. The AC is subsequently turned on when people enter the room and vice versa.

Environmental Friendly

The auto-control of AC reduces energy waste led by forgetting to turn it off, and thus contribute to the protection of the environment in the long run.

Internet of Things

Raspberry Pi provides real-time temperature and humidity data, which are automatically updated to this website. Conversely, users can remotely set up the humidity and temperature, and the AC will adjust correspondingly.
